About This Surah

Hud is the surah the Prophet ﷺ said gave him grey hair, due to the verse 'Stand firm as you are commanded.' It presents the longest sequence of prophetic narratives so far — Nuh, Hud, Salih, Ibrahim, Lut, Shu'ayb, and Musa — each following the same pattern: a sincere prophet, an arrogant people, a clear warning, and decisive divine judgment. The surah's purpose is to console the Prophet ﷺ and Muslims facing rejection in Mecca, showing that this pattern is universal and that ultimate victory belongs to those who hold firm.
