About This Surah

Fatir emphasizes that humans are poor (fuqara') and Allah is the Rich (Ghani). It uses signs from nature — wind, rain, the different colors of mountains — to show the diversity of divine creation and the truth of resurrection.

Names of Allah Mentioned

Fatir (The Originator)Al-Ghani (The Free of Need)Al-Ghafur (The Forgiving)

Key Terms

Fuqara'
